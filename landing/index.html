How it works

Three steps to owning your career story

Winsync works passively in the background — no habit change required.

1

Label an email in Gmail

Received a compliment, positive feedback, or recognition? Apply the winsync label to it in Gmail. That's it.

2

AI extracts your win

Our AI reads the email, extracts the key achievement or feedback, and adds it to your personal Digest as a pending item.

3

Review day is effortless

When review season comes, select a date range and Winsync generates a structured, polished self-review draft in seconds.
